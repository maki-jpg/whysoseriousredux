Discovery

June 16, 2008

The Citizens for Batman Underground was a hidden forum on the Citizensforbatman.org server, set up by several members and publicized through the Gotham City Pizzeria's free pizza promotion, unbeknowst to the organization's moderators, to discuss becoming vigilantes.

To enter the underground at citizensforbatman.org/midnight/nyctalus/threat/plerotes/history/euderma/fear/myotis/underestimation:

Username: nycticeius (or nycticeious)
Password: merritt

All the threads are considered relevant to the investigation, and are listed below. They can also be read on the site mirror.
