Discovery

June 27, 2008

Whysoserious.com was found updated to include a montage of various Gotham websites, and a checklist with links to every major Joker trail. The accomplished tasks up to that point were crossed out on the list, with clues to three future ones added to the bottom. As they were fulfilled, each task was crossed out in turn.
