Discovery

March 30, 2008

From clues called in to participants with Joker cell phones, Clowntravelagency.com was discovered. Upon discovery, the site displayed a suitcase and an envelope with "DEPARTURE DATE 4-01" written.

Top

Assignment

April 1, 2008

At 9AM EST, the image was replaced with an identical one, except with the envelope open.

Upon "opening" the envelope, a message was shown:

Ready to have a ball?

A special bag of fun awaits the first to claim it, but you'd better strike fast, there's no time to spare!

These locations were bowling alleys, since the note mentioned "ball," "strike," and "spare." The US locations were a locker number with a combination.

When a location was visited and the task completed, a postmark was stamped onto the address.

Top

Reward

The reward was a leather bag containing a green-and-purple bowling ball and evidence bag. A "clown" name and a phone number were engraved onto the ball. The evidence bag contained a cell phone, charger, Joker card, and a message from the Joker instructing the participant to call the number on the ball to complete the task.




Nice work, clown! Now call the number on the ball immediately, from this phone and THIS PHONE ONLY. Don't give this number to anyone else, or I'll know. Hopefully, your cohorts will be in the same league as you, because once all your points are racked up, I'll give you all something that will really bowl you over...

As in the previous phone hunt, upon calling the phone, a woman's voice on the other end cheerily said:

Thank you for calling Rent-a-Clown! Hahahaha! We now know who you are! Good-bye!

Top

Result

Once all of the locations had been accounted for, the page loaded with all of the postmarks; then the list burned away to reveal a congratulatory note from the Joker.

Good job, clowns!

You've proven you're kingpin material, so I've got a job that's right up your alley.

Head over to www.acmesecuritysystems.com/delos and see if you can disable a certain alarm system for me.

The password is: needle.
Be sure not to use a phony email address.

It'll be OK?. I promise.
