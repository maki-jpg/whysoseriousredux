At Gotham Cab, we seek to provide you with personalized service.
About Us:
Gotham Cab Company is now open under new management. We've put the unfortunate incidents of the past behind us and pledge to support Gotham City in all of its efforts against organized crime. So let us take you on a ride!


The Numbers: Gotham Cab has over 150 taxis on the city streets at any one time. Every week, we help over 5,000 Gothamites get where they need to go. And every month, we drive over 50,000 miles of Gotham streets!
We Care: Gotham Cab continues to offer free taxi service within The Narrows and the Gotham City Rail destruction zone. Just present your transfer ticket to any Gotham Cab. If you need pick-up, give us a call!
