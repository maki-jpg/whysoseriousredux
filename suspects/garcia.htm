Anthony Garcia

Mayor of Gotham City

Review:

Anthony Garcia is the mayor of Gotham City, currently serving his second term after being re-elected on a platform to clean up crime and corruption. To that end, he appointed James Gordon as the head of the newly-formed Major Crimes Unit, and later appointed him to the rank of Commissioner.
